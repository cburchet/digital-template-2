2 player Breakout

  • High Concept: Player vs computer game of breakout with multiple levels increasing in difficulty.
  • Player Experience Goals: The feeling of competition against the computer as they try to beat it as well as competition against friends as they try to reach the highest level possible.
  • Genre: Arcade style game
  • Game play: Use the mouse to move the paddle to bounce the ball back at the opponent and eliminate all of their bricks. One of the aspects of gameplay that I did not get to implementing would be power ups for the ball to access such as temporary increase in speed, massive curve, etc.
  • Prototype goal: Two players playing breakout with one of the players being controlled by the computer. Multiple levels can be played with a better computer and more bricks to destroy.
  • Features: 2 players including a computer player.
  • Setting/Premise: Just two people playing breakout against each other
  • Story: NA
  • Target Audience: Competitive players
  • Play time: A few minutes perhaps longer.
  • Strategies: Bouncing the ball at a curve (not hitting the ball in the center of the paddle) will likely be the best way to score.
